The indoor climbing manager for setters, climbers, and gyms.

Setter allows you to track of your setting history and get feedback from your climbers, gaining you valuable insight to better serve your customers.

Setter for Gyms

  • Track your setting history
  • Receive feedback on routes & problems from your climbers
  • Alert climbers of new routes, upcoming competitions, events or special hours
  • Archive your bouldering, top rope, and lead sets for future reference, such as when scheduling setters.

Setter for Climbers

  • Track your setting history
  • Receive feedback on routes & problems from your climbers
  • Alert climbers of new routes, upcoming competitions, events or special hours
  • Archive your bouldering, top rope, and lead sets for future reference, such as when scheduling setters.
